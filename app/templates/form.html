What is this transmitter?

This device is a low-powered radio transmitter for Amateur Radio Direction Finding (ARDF), also known as fox hunting. This device transmits on 144.565 MHz FM. Amateur Radio Operators use specialized equipment to track and locate this device for training purposes.

This device is operated by the UT Austin Amateur Radio Club, callsign N5XU. Please do not touch or remove this device.
